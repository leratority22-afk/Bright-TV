Bright TV: Sales & Product Performance Case Study
This repository contains a data analytics case study focused on the sales performance, product trends, and revenue growth of Bright TV. The project involves analyzing transaction data to identify top-performing products, regional sales trends, and customer buying patterns.

Project Background
Bright TV is a television retailer looking to optimize its inventory and marketing strategies. This analysis aims to answer critical business questions regarding product category performance, store efficiency, and revenue seasonality.

Objectives
Revenue Analysis: Calculate total sales and identify growth trends over specific periods.

Product Performance: Determine which TV models and categories (e.g., Smart TVs, OLED, LED) are driving the most profit.

Regional Insights: Compare performance across different store locations or regions.

Customer Behavior: Analyze average transaction values and quantity per order.

Technical Stack
Data Processing: Excel / CSV

Analysis: SQL Fundamentals (Aggregates, Grouping, CASE Statements)

Documentation: Markdown

Data Overview
The analysis is based on two primary data sources:

Transaction Records: A detailed log of sales including transaction_id, product_name, category, price, and quantity.

Performance Results: A compiled set of results from SQL queries and data summaries focusing on high-level KPIs.

Key Insights
Top Products: Identification of the highest-grossing television models.

Category Leaders: Comparison of different technology types (e.g., 4K vs. 8K).

Sales Distribution: A breakdown of how revenue is distributed across different customer segments or timeframes.
